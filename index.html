Video Consultation

During the exploration for building SUSHAIN platform, we came across the availability of Excellent Ayurvedic Practitioners across the country. We found specialists with credible success stories in treating even fatal diseases, the challenge was Accessibility for larger population- Hence we decided to bring them on a state-of-the-art video consultation platform, wherein by a click of link both doctor and patients can interact. Patient gets a digital prescription and digital records of his medical history.

ORDER MEDICINE

After building a video consultation platform, the next challenge was to build an echo system to provide authentic Ayurvedic medicines to our patients. With the same agenda we decided built an in-house pharmacy under the leadership of Dr Rajesh Singh, to provide trustworthy ayurvedic combinations to our patients as prescribed by the physicians. We also tied up with Authentic Ayurvedic medicine manufacturers to become one stop platform for all the branded Ayurvedic Medicine brands. To add-up we decided to have some of the frequently used Ayurvedic medicines in its purest extract form under sushain brand.

YOGA AND PANCHKARMA

Yoga & Panchmarma are complimentary Ayurveda practices to increase the pace of the treatment. On sushain platform you can book personalized yoga classes where our trained Yoga Practitioner can train you on Yoga and meditation specific to your health concern.
You can also book a panchkarma treatment on our verified panchkarma centers basis the recommendation of your Ayurvedic doctor.

Clinic Consultation

While providing the online consultation, we are also trying to make the local accessibility of Ayurvedic doctors convenient for the patients. They can easily search the doctors in their city, filter as per their health concern and book an appointment.
